About Zac Poonen

Zac Poonen was formerly an Indian Naval Officer who has been serving the Lord in India for over 50 years as a Bible-teacher. He has responsibility for a number of churches in India and abroad.

He has written more than 25 books and numerous articles in English - which have been translated into many Indian and foreign languages. His messages are available on audio CDs and video DVDs.

Like the other elders in CFC, Zac Poonen also supports himself and his family through " tent-making" and does not receive any salary for his services. He does not receive any royalty for any of his books, CDs or DVDs that are published by Christian Fellowship Center, Bangalore.
